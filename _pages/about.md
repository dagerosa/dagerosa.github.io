My name is Daniele Gerosa, and since 2024 I am a postdoctoral fellow at Chalmers University of Techonology, Göteborg (Sweden). I belong to the group of [Thomas Eriksson](https://www.chalmers.se/en/persons/thomase/), but I collaborate also with [Mats Viberg](https://www.bth.se/english/staff-landing-page/mats-viberg) and [Tomas McKelvey](https://www.chalmers.se/en/persons/mckelvey/). I received the Ph.D in mathematics from Lund University (Sweden) in 2022, under the supervision of [Marcus Carlsson](https://www.lunduniversity.lu.se/lucat/user/7240ab1d6ba79a9dafc458e7602b150e), and the M.Sc in mathematics from University of Padova (Italy) in 2017, under the supervision of [Roberto Monti](https://www.math.unipd.it/~monti/index.html).

Between 2022 and 2024 I was with Ericsson AB, Göteborg, in the algorithm development team. 

My research interests are compressive sensing, applied and pure sampling theory, statistical signal processing for communication and radar applications, and more broadly mathematics for signal processing.
